
# Code of Conduct

We are dedicated to providing a respectful, inclusive, and harassment‑free experience for everyone participating in the UniLog Toolkit community.

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers, pledge to foster a welcoming and safe environment for all—regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or socio‑economic status.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behaviors include:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment or doxxing
- Publishing others’ private information without explicit permission
- Sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Other conduct that is inappropriate for a professional setting

## Scope
This Code applies within all project spaces—GitHub issues, discussions, pull requests, code reviews, and community channels—as well as in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at **harrisw@athabascau.ca**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

- Enforcement actions may include a warning, temporary or permanent ban from the community, or other steps deemed appropriate.
- Maintainers will respect the privacy and security of the reporter of an incident.
